La Croix Drop Performance Analysis

Promotion Period: Sept 22 - Sept 29 | Analysis Period: Pre Aug 18 - Sept 19th vs Post Oct 6 - Nov 3

Please Note: The total number of users represents a subset of participants in the drop—specifically, those who had a Just Salad account prior to 1/1/2025. This subset performed statistically similar to the overall participant group, indicating that its exclusion did not skew the metrics.
Data limited to 1st Party Orders only

Key Takeaways

1.

Of users that participated in the drop, the La Croix attach on their orders went from 5% to 8% between the pre and the post period. This represents a 3% increase in attach rate among drop participants' orders.

2.

Of existing customers who redeemed the La Croix drop, 12% went on to purchase the product in the post-drop period, demonstrating strong conversion from trial to purchase behavior.
