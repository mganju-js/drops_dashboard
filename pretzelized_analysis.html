Pretzelized Promotion Performance Analysis

Promotion Period: October 27 - November 3, 2024 | Analysis Period: Pre (9/15-10/13) vs Post (11/10-12/08)
Data limited to app and web channel orders only

Key Takeaways

1.

Orders containing pretzelized items increased by only 2% post-promotion among users who participated in the drop. This represents a significant performance gap compared to La Croix, which achieved a 5% increase in orders containing the promoted item—suggesting this promotion was less effective at converting trial users into paying customers.

2.

Of existing customers who redeemed the pretzelized promotion, only 7.4% went on to purchase the product in the post-drop period. This conversion rate is 39% lower than La Croix's 12.14%, further indicating underperformance relative to previous promotional campaigns.
