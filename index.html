Pretzelized Drop Performance Analysis

Promotion Period: Oct 27 - Nov 3, 2025 | Analysis Period: Pre Sept 15 - Oct 13, 2025 vs Post Nov 10 - Dec 8, 2025

Please Note: The total number of users represents a subset of participants in the drop—specifically, those who had a Just Salad account prior to 1/1/2025. This subset performed statistically similar to the overall participant group, indicating that its exclusion did not skew the metrics.
Data limited to 1st Party Orders only

Key Takeaways

1.

Of users that participated in the drop, the Pretzelized attach on their orders went from 2% to 4% between the pre and the post period. This is a performance gap compared to La Croix, which went from 5% to 8% attach among drop participant's orders between the pre and post period.

2.

Of existing customers who redeemed the Pretzelized drop, only 7% went on to purchase the product in the post-drop period. This is lower than La Croix drop where 12% of users that redeemed the drop purchased the product in the post period, indicating underperformance relative to previous drop campaigns.
